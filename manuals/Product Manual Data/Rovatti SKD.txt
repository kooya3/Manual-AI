PUMP
The ROVATTI SKD range of horizontal multistage centrifugal pumps are heavy duty-high pressure industrial quality pumps suitable for various
water supply, irrigation and industrial applications. They feature double bearing support and outlet with upwards delivery flange. Suction flange is
settable to 3 different positions by rotating through 900 for SKD 150 Series. Specification include gland packing seals for simple and economical
maintenance and external securing tie bolts for ease of disassembly. Construction material of the heavy duty spheroidal end pieces and stages as
well as diffusers is cast iron body. Impellers are made from Cast Iron treated with cataphoresis apart from Brass for SKD65-22.
A wide range of models and specifications are available, a combination of the pump size and number of stages being selected to achieve
maximum efficiency at a specific duty point. Electric motors are selected according to the required power input at the duty point and mounted
together with the pump and coupling on a base frame. All pumps are also suitable for direct or belt drive by diesel engines
OPERATING CONDITIONS
Pumped Liquid: Thin, Clean, chemically non-aggressive liquids without particles or fibres
Maximum Solid Content: 40g/m3
Max. Fluid Temperature: 900C
Maximum Running Time With Zero Flow: 2 minutes
Max. Suction Pressure: 16 bar
Max. Delivery Pressure: 50 Bar (SKD65/80/100), 40 Bar (SKD150/200)
Rotation: Clockwise Viewed from driving end
