Fountain
Jets & Nozzles
Dayliff jets and nozzles are precision made products that transform any garden
into a masterpiece of art. They are made from stainless steel except
Schaumsprundler which is made from chrome. The display size is determined
by water flow and pressure suitable pumps being available according to
required display effect.
