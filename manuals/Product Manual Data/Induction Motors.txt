DAYLIFF TEFC squirrel cage induction motors are high specification continuous duty rated machines suitable for a wide variety of industrial, commercial
and agricultural applications. A particular feature is high operating efficiency with the specification meeting the demanding IE3 standards, other features
including Class F insulation, low noise, high starting torque and all dimensions are fully compliant to IEC standards. DAYLIFF motors are also of extremely
robust design providing high levels of serviceability and reliability.
