Pool Safety
Cover

Safety around swimming pools is a major concern, especially for residential
sites where children and pets have access. DAYLIFF safety cover addresses this
problem. The simple, easy to install system provides peace of mind to pool
owners and operators.
The safety cover is made of strong UV polypropylene mesh and is retained by
anchor hooks around the pool edge. This simple arrangement is suitable for all
pool shapes and provides for easy fixing and removal.
DAYLIFF safety covers are simple and effective and provide excellent security in
all installations where user safety is a concern.
